Ashtanna

Ashtanna is the engineering assistant on the Antigoni. She is very capable with the Magic aspects of things, but very weak in the Tech side. Her ability to intuit the Magic is almost as great as Anta's because of her spiritual connections. She worships the ground the twins walk on because of their incredible skills.

She is the latest addition to the crew.

Ashtanna was abused by her parents and the Temple once they discovered she was a lesbian. The Temple tried to cure her at the request of her parents. This was done using psychological and physical torments, includiong ritualized rape, intended to reveal the wrongness of her beliefs. Ashtanna hates the Temple and has disavowed her parents. She never wears anything that will reveal her back or shoulders because they would show the scars from the tortures used by the Temple.

She dares not go city-side in places that are dominated by Templars because of how she left her home city.

She is spiritual, in her own way. She believes the life forms of Hexiaz are connected in a web of thought and energy that creates a 'god' that lives in the clouds. She is happiest when the ship is out in the clouds, away from the chaos and noise of the Hexiazi.
